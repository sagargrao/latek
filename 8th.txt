\section{Examples}

\begin{theorem}[Pythagoras Theorem]
\label{Pythagoras}

In a right angled triangle, the square of hypotenuse is equal to the sum of squares of the other two sides.

\end{theorem}

\begin{lemma}

Pythagoras was a mathematician.

\end{lemma}

\begin{corollary}

The Pythagoras theorem is useful in geometry.

\end{corollary}

You can reference theorem~\ref{pythagoras}.

\begin{definition}

A right angled triangle is a triangle in which one angle is equal to $90^\circ$.

\end{definition}
